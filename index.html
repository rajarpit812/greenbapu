Who We Are

Inspired by the principles of Mahatma Gandhi, Green Bapu was founded in 2018 with a simple yet powerful vision: to mobilize communities for environmental action. We believe that small, collective efforts can lead to massive change. Our work is rooted in the idea of "Sarvodaya" – progress for all, which includes the well-being of our planet.

We are a passionate team of environmentalists, volunteers, and citizens from across India, committed to tackling pressing issues like deforestation, pollution, and waste management. Our mission is to restore ecological balance through hands-on projects and awareness campaigns.

Our Core Initiatives

We focus on critical areas where we can make the most significant impact on India's environment.

Tree Plantation Drives

We organize mass tree plantation drives in urban and rural areas to combat deforestation, improve air quality, and restore biodiversity. Every sapling planted is a step towards a greener tomorrow.

River & Lake Cleanup

Our volunteers work tirelessly to clean polluted rivers and lakes, removing plastic and other waste. We aim to restore our precious water bodies to their natural, pristine state.

Waste Management & Recycling

We promote sustainable waste management practices and set up community recycling programs. Our goal is to reduce landfill burden and create a circular economy for waste.
